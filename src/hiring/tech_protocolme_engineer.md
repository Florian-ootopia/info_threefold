- technical

  - fluent in at least 2 development languages
  - fluent with github, agile processes
  - can work with docker or alike
- professional skills 
  - fluent in English
  - good communicator
  - understand 20-80 procent rule: pareto

  - don't be afraid of changes

  - understand quality is important
